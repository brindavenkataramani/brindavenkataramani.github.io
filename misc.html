Quantum mechanics with sets?

Okay, so this is a completely silly, outlandish, absurd idea, but if we assume GCH, then sets are discrete (or at least cardinalities after \(\omega$ are\). Perhaps we can operate on the continuum of cardinalities instead of in units of \(\hbar\)? Alternatively, we can just do functional analysis with sets provided we find a suitable functor to push sets onto infinite dimensional vector spaces (or rather pullback said category onto Set).
