Some division proofs

This section is dedicated to some proofs involving the divisibility of integers.

  1. If \(a\vert b\), and \(b\vert c\), then \(a\vert c\).
    If \(a\vert b\), then \(b=am,\,m\in\mathbb{Z}\).
