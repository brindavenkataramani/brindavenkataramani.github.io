Potential energy of spring

An ideal spring exerts force, \(F=-kx\) on a point-like masss. To move a mass, $m$ from equilibrium to a point $x$, we exert a constant force, $F=kx$. Potential energy of an ideal spring is hence given by, $$\int_0^xkx\,dx=\frac{1}{2}kx^2.$$
