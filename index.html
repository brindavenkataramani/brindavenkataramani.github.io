  1. Potential energy of a spring

Potential energy of spring

An ideal spring exerts force, \(F=-kx\) on a mass. To move a mass, \(m\) from equilibrium to a point \(x\), we exert a constant force, \(F=kx\). Potential energy of an ideal spring is hence given by, $$\int_0^xkx\,dx=\frac{1}{2}kx^2.$$
